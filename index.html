Our Mission

To build a platform where students can learn beyond the classroom, participate in tech competition, and collaborate in building real projects for the community, together.

  • Join us

Our Activities

  • Workshops

    We organise workshops that teach essential skills which may not be taught in your classroom. Learn how to build your own apps and the development tools that is used in the actual industry.

  • Competitions

    We participate in competitions such as Hackathons (building apps) and CTFs (hacking) to test our skills and promote our growth.

  • Projects

    We collaborate with each other to apply our knowledge by building real projects that can benefits the local community.

Our Achievements

  • APU Battle of Hackers - Rank 9

    We managed to be the finalist and obtained the 9th rank in APU Battle of Hackers CTF (Capture The Flag). CTF is a hacking/cybersecurity competition that involves many security fields such as Forensics, Reverse Engineering, Cryptography, and Binary Exploitation.

  • iUM Disrupt - 2nd Place

    We won 2nd place in iUM Disrupt Hackathon 2020. Hackathon is a competition where you need to build a solution and pitch it under a limited time (24 Hr). We built a COVID-19 pre-assessment app with appointment system and E-records.

Why Join UTP ADC?

If you are passionate about tech and looking to improve your skill, join competitions, or just want to build apps, we are the right platform for you!
